Coding Temple Goals



I am hoping to gain the skills necessary to become a software engineer. Not only do I love working in tech, but I've always
loved computers, and immersing myself in a coding bootcamp will give me the skills necessary to begin my career as a SWE.


While I've dabbled in online courses over the years (and even considered minoring in CS back in college), I've never taken
the full leap and committed myself to learning how to code. I learn best in a structured environment, which is why an immersive
bootcamp program like Coding Temple is a perfect fit for me.


I'm extremely excited to continue my journey on the path to becoming a software engineer. Coding Temple will accelerate my
learning by allowing me to focus purely on the program itself, and when I come out of it, I will be thinking and learning like
a software engineer would.





Beyond the Program



I fully anticipate getting a software engineering job sometime after the completion of my bootcamp. In an ideal world,
I will get a SWE job at my current company. But if I have to leave to start my programming career elsewhere, then that is exactly
what I'll do.


I'm not too worried about how long it may take me to find a job in this industry. I understand that entry-level SWE jobs are harder to
come by in 2023 then they were in 2020 or even 2021. I plan on working very hard to improve my skills so that when the right
opportunity comes along, I will look like the very best candidate for the job


Freelance coding and consulting is an excellent way to work on my skills as a programmer after the bootcamp (and before landing that
first SWE job). Not only will it build my resume and improve my abilities, but it could blossom into a legitimate side-hustle, depending
of course on what route I decide to take.


Overall, I am just extremely excited to get going with this next phase in my career. It was a blast throwing together this portfolio website, and I
can't wait to see what I will be capable of creating down the line!
